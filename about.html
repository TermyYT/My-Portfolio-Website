About Me

Good day to you, ladies and gentlemen! My name is David, I'm 23, an IT major, and I graduated from NJIT in 2024!

Quick Facts

  • My interests include: gaming, technology, coding, philosophy, the arts, and music.
  • I describe myself as a naturally caring and compassionate person.
  • I'm a very optimistic, but also realistic, and typically happy person. I like to look on the bright side and I have hope for the future.
  • I talk a lot, so if you catch my interest with something, prepare for walls of text due to my overflowing enthusiasm.
  • I'm an INFJ if you want to get a general feel of my personality, although Myers-Briggs type isn't important to me.
  • My dream is simply to help others and make people feel happy and good about themselves. I consider a day a success if I'm able to do this for even one person. It brings me a sense of purpose and fulfillment.
  • I'm willing to talk about most things!
  • I am an excellent active listener and enjoy speaking with compassion in mind.

Education

I have a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology from the New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT) which I acquired in 2024. It was one of my proudest moments in my academic career, and while it took many years to get, it was well worth it in the end. During my time at NJIT, I took a large variety of classes which allowed me to become well-versed in all things technology-related. I learned many valuable skills which would later become highly relevant and applicable in my career as a software developer, and I'm grateful for the opportunities I was given by my professors.

Interests

For as long as I could remember, I had been playing video games. The earliest I remember playing a video game was at the age of 5. Since then, my experience with games has only grown. The games I played as a small child still hold a special place in my heart nearly 2 decades later. Now, due to my upbringing, gaming has become one of my favorite hobbies. These days, I prefer indie titles because I believe that's where the passion in gaming truly lies. In my humble opinion, AAA titles no longer hold the magic they once did.

Over the years of playing games, I had also picked up on various nuances of the Windows operating system, how software works, how to write better, etc. As time went on and I became more well-versed in computers, my interest and fascination in them grew. Now, I can't imagine living life without computers. Overall, discovering the depth of computers was one of the most important journeys of my life. I knew from a young age that I wanted to work with computers.

Discord Server

My primary passion project is the Discord server I have been continuously developing with close friends since 2019 called "Paradigm Shifters". It's a public community server with a strong focus on love, compassion, appreciation, and positivity. Through it, I have met and grown close with many individuals from across the globe and have learned a lot about the value of friendship. Now, it's where I can be found most of the time, enjoying others' company, fostering a positive and loving environment, playing games, hanging out, and making new connections. I could not be more grateful for this online community and the many friendships I made through it.
